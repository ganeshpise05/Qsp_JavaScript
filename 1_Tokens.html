    VARIABLE SCOPE

    1. Global Scope

      The var which are accessible inside the class & outside the class is called Global scope var.

    2. Local scope

      The var which are accessible within the block & they are not accesible outside is called Local/Block scope var.

      The var declared by var are accessible outside the block. The var declared by let & const are not accessible outside the block.
